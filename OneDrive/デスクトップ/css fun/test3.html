Doug Kowalski is a passionate photographer known for capturing the quiet beauty in everyday moments. With a keen eye for detail and composition, he often explores nature, urban life, and stillness through his lens. His photography reflects a deep appreciation for light, texture, and atmosphere—turning simple scenes into compelling visual stories.

One of Doug’s most striking photos features a single droplet resting delicately on a vibrant green leaf. Taken during an early morning walk after a light rain, the image captures the purity and stillness of the moment. The reflection inside the droplet reveals a tiny, upside-down world, making the photo not only beautiful but also deeply contemplative.
